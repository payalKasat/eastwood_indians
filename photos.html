Eastwood School


Eastwood Village opening in fall 2017, the new elementary school is conveniently located in the village center, so families can walk and bike to school. 3 parks are surrounding school premises. School including dedicated music classrooms, innovation labs, design labs, a kindergarten play area, large outdoor play field and student collaboration spaces across all grades.

Eucalyptus park


Masterfully organized and well maintain park, located on center of eastwood. Eucalyptus park have 2-5 and 5-12 years old playing equipments. Also have basketball court and community hall. This park have 2 swimming pools and 1 jacuzzi pool.

Trail near eastwood


Beautiful biking,walking, running, jogging and playing open space within a mail away. Beautiful landscaping and more than 1000s of trees makes it more fresh and lively.

Shopping dinning



Shopping is right around the corner. Woodbuary town center, culver shopping center and cypress shopping center are within a 2 miles away. 3 different pharmacy, car wash and gasoline options right next door.
